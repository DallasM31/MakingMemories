Welcome!

Making Memories is our vacation rental condo with 3 bedrooms and an unenclosed loft (sleeps 13 easily), 4.5 bathrooms furnished with all the upgrades you could ask for, including flat-screen HDTV's in every room, granite counter-tops, as well as stainless steel appliances.

Whether you're looking to relax or spend your day enjoying the surrounding landscapes, you can rest comfortably in the contemporary style bedrooms, partially covered outdoor patio, or spend time with family and friends in our large family room.

The Ledges of St. George is the perfect area to visit for golfers and outdoor enthusiasts. Minutes away you'll find a conglomerate of hiking, biking, horseback and ATV trails, world famous rock climbing and repelling, and views of the majestic red and white sandstone mountains surrounding The Ledges.
